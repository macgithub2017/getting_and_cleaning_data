# CodeBook

# Output file variables

## Subject and Activity; Column 1 and 2, respectively.

 - Subject, subject ID. From 1 to 30 subjects.(Integer)
 - Activity, named as follow (Character):
   - `Walking`
   - `Walking Upstairs`
   - `Walking Downstairs` 
   - `Sitting`
   - `Standing`
   - `Laying`
